About Me

I'm a passionate Computer Science student at the University of Washington, dedicated to exploring the endless possibilities of technology and innovation.

My Story

My journey into computer science began during my junior year of high school when I took AP Computer Science A. What captivated me most was the unique way of thinking required when programming—the precision, logic, and creativity needed to communicate with computers compared to human interaction.

Now as a Computer Science student at the University of Washington (Class of 2027), I'm constantly expanding my knowledge and skills. I love tackling challenging problems, building innovative projects, and exploring new technologies. Each day brings new opportunities to learn and grow in this ever-evolving field.

Beyond coding, I believe in the power of technology to make a positive impact on the world. Whether it's creating engaging user experiences, developing efficient algorithms, or collaborating with others, I'm driven by the desire to build meaningful solutions.
